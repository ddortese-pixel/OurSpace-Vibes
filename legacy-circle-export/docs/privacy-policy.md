# Privacy Policy — The Legacy Circle

**Effective Date:** April 10, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 10, 2026

## Overview

The Legacy Circle ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use The Legacy Circle mobile and web application.

We comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)**, and applicable international privacy laws.

---

## Who We Are

The Legacy Circle is an AI-powered educational platform designed for children ages 2–14. Parents or legal guardians create and manage accounts on behalf of their children.

---

## Information We Collect

### From Parents / Guardians
- Email address (for account creation and notifications)
- Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers — we never store card details)

### From Children (with parental consent)
- Display name and age range
- Learning progress, XP, badges, and story choices
- Quiz and mission results
- In-app messages (Glow Messages — moderated)
- Device type and general usage data

### Automatically Collected
- App usage analytics (sessions, features used, time spent)
- Device type and operating system
- Crash reports

---

## How We Use Information

We use collected data to:
- Personalize the learning experience for each child
- Track progress and award achievements
- Send parent notifications (level-ups, badges, streaks)
- Improve app features and content
- Ensure platform safety and prevent misuse

We **never** sell personal information. We **never** use children's data for targeted advertising.

---

## AI Interactions

The Legacy Circle uses AI to power character conversations and story branching. All AI interactions are:
- Filtered for age-appropriate content
- Logged for safety monitoring
- Never used to build advertising profiles

---

## Parental Rights (COPPA)

Parents and guardians have the right to:
- Review their child's personal information
- Request deletion of their child's data
- Refuse further data collection
- Set screen time limits and notification preferences within the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@thelegacycircle.com**

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share data with:
- **Service providers** who help operate the app (hosting, analytics) — bound by confidentiality agreements
- **Law enforcement** if required by law

---

## Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect all user data. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

---

## Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify parents via email of any material changes.
