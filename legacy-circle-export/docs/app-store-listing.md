## Full Description

Meet The Legacy Circle — where kids don't just learn, they level up.

The Legacy Circle is an adaptive, AI-powered learning ecosystem designed for children ages 2–14. Through immersive storytelling, gamified missions, and meaningful character interactions, kids build the skills that matter most: emotional intelligence, digital literacy, and community leadership.

**Why parents love it:**
- Safe, age-appropriate AI interactions guided by trusted characters
- Real-time progress tracking and parent notifications
- Daily family conversation prompts (Dinner Table Prompts)
- Screen time limits and parental controls built in
- No ads. Ever.

**Why kids love it:**
- Choose your own story paths and shape the world around you
- Earn XP, badges, and unlock exclusive items in the marketplace
- Send Glow Messages to cheer on friends
- Compete in Weekly Challenges and Seasonal Events
- Meet and interview iconic characters from history and culture

**Learning that sticks:**
Every mission, story, and quiz is designed around proven competency frameworks. Kids earn Mastery badges when they demonstrate real understanding — not just right answers.

**Built for every child:**
Adaptive age modes ensure the experience is always just right, whether your child is 2 or 14.
