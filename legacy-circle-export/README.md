# The Legacy Circle

An adaptive, AI-powered learning ecosystem that helps children ages 2–14 build emotional intelligence, digital literacy, and community leadership through interactive storytelling and gamified missions.

## App Structure

This repository contains the exported schema and structure of The Legacy Circle Base44 app.

### Entities
- **LearnerProfile** — Core learner data including XP, badges, streaks, and preferences
- **MarketplaceItem** — Items available in the in-app marketplace
- **PurchasedItem** — Items purchased by learners
- **StoryProgress** — Tracks learner progress through story episodes
- **MasteryQuiz** — Quiz results and competency tracking
- **GlowMessage** — Peer-to-peer encouragement messages between learners
- **WeeklyChallenge** — Weekly challenge tracking and rewards
- **SeasonalEvent** — Seasonal events and community goals
- **EventContribution** — Individual contributions to seasonal events
- **StoreProduct** — Products available in the store
- **AppNotification** — In-app notifications for users
- **StoryChatLog** — AI character chat session logs
- **UnlockedMuseumItem** — Museum items unlocked by learners
- **StoryFeed** — Community story feed posts
- **StoryFeedReply** — Replies to story feed posts
- **DinnerTablePrompt** — Daily family conversation prompts
- **CharacterInterview** — AI character interview sessions
