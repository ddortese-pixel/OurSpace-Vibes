## Full Description

### TIRED OF BEING MANIPULATED?
Every major social platform uses your data, your behavior, and your emotions to keep you scrolling. OurSpace Vibes was built as the antidote.

**10,000+ people have already made the switch.**

---

### THE UNDERGROUND FEED 📰
Your feed is 100% chronological. No algorithm decides what you see — you follow people, and you see their posts. That's it. No boosted content, no paid reach, no invisible ranking.

### THE SHIELD — E2EE MESSAGING 🔒
Your private conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We literally cannot read them. Not even our engineers. Your messages belong to you.

### HUMAN-ONLY FILTER ✅
Toggle on the Human-Only Filter to see only verified human-created content. AI-generated posts must be labeled — no fake authenticity on OurSpace.

### YOUR DIGITAL SPACE 🎨
Your profile is your corner of the internet. Choose your theme, set your mood, share your playlist, let friends leave messages on your guestbook wall.

### SERIALIZED STORIES 📖
Follow creators through episodes and series. Think a TV show, but social. Subscribe to creators and watch their story unfold over time.

---

### PRIVACY BY DESIGN
- **No selling your data.** Ever.
- **No targeted advertising.** Your behavior is not a product.
- **No algorithmic profiling.** We don't track what you engage with to manipulate you.
- **GDPR + CCPA + COPPA compliant.**
- **Age-verified.** 13+ required. Parental consent for under-18.
